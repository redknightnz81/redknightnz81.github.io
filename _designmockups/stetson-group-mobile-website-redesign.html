This redesign started out by the site owner asking what could be done about their website to make it more 'mobile friendly'.

I have used the colours from the main site, and kept the large banner image for upcoming shows.

Also contains a long list of upcoming and past shows as per the full site.

Only this home page was made in the mockup, as shortly after they informed me that they had found someone else to undertake a 'mobile friendly' update to their website.
